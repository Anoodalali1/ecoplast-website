About EcoPlast

Our Mission

EcoPlast was founded by a young Emirati chemical engineer who started in a small workshop in Umm Al Quwain with a clear goal: turn used plastic into reliable, beautiful, and affordable recycled products. We believe sustainability should be practical and accessible — rooted in science, powered by community, and proudly made in the UAE.

Today, EcoPlast transforms locally collected plastic into durable packaging people use every day — from garbage bags to shrink film and polythene sleeves. By recycling close to home, we cut transport emissions, create local jobs, and keep valuable materials in circulation.

Why Recycled Plastic?

Plastic waste is one of the biggest environmental challenges of our time. By choosing recycled plastic, you help reduce landfill waste, conserve natural resources, and lower the carbon footprint associated with new plastic production — without compromising on performance.

Our Commitment

  • Use of 100% recycled materials across our product lines
  • Fair bulk pricing to make sustainable choices easy
  • Quality that performs as well as virgin plastic
  • Support for a circular economy in the UAE

Our Products

We make three essentials: heavy‑duty garbage bags, versatile shrink film plastic bags, and clear polythene bags. Every product is available at bulk pricing of $9.99 per 100 pieces.
