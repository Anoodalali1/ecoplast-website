Frequently Asked Questions

What products do you offer?

We offer three types of recycled plastic products designed for everyday use. Our product line includes plastic garbage bags for effective waste management, shrink film plastic bags for versatile packaging needs, and polythene bags for general-purpose use. All products are made from 100% recycled materials and maintain the same quality and durability as products made from virgin plastic.

What is your pricing?

All our products are uniformly priced at $9.99 for a pack of 100 pieces. This bulk pricing structure makes our recycled products affordable and accessible for both businesses and individual consumers.

Are your products really made from recycled plastic?

Yes! All our products are manufactured using 100% recycled plastic materials. By using recycled plastic, we help reduce landfill waste, conserve natural resources, and decrease the carbon footprint associated with producing new plastic from petroleum.

How do I place an order?

Contact us via phone at (608) 695-0338 or email at asalali3@wisc.edu. Let us know which products you need and the quantity, and we will process your order promptly.

Do you offer discounts for large orders?

Yes! For orders exceeding certain quantities, we offer additional discounts. Please contact us directly to discuss your specific needs and receive a custom quote.

How durable are recycled plastic bags compared to new plastic?

Our recycled plastic bags are just as durable and reliable as bags made from virgin plastic. They meet the same quality standards and perform equally well in everyday use.

What is the minimum order quantity?

Our minimum order is one pack of 100 pieces for any of our three product types.

How long does shipping take?

Once payment is confirmed, we process orders promptly and ship them as quickly as possible. Shipping times vary by location; tracking is provided on dispatch.

Have More Questions?

We are here to help! Contact us anytime for more information about our products, pricing, or ordering process.
